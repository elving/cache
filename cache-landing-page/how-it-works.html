How it works

Collections

Collections are containers for your items. They describe what a group of items is about and provide smart ways to filter and search items.

Create a collection

Collections are defined by their title, description and icon.

Create a collection

Items

Items are links, text, images, videos and audio you store in your collections.

Unlike traditional bookmarks, items in Cache are more than links, they are content from around the web.

Cache integrates with your favorite services and previews the items you collect so that you don't have to leave your collection.

See the full list of integrations

Store items

You can store links from your collections, but the best way to collect links with Cache is with the browser extension.

Install the extension

With the browser extension you can collect links with one click. You can also collect text and images from any website; just right-click on the content you want to collect.
